## 2. Data Sources and Proprietary Data

### What VYRION Uses

All VYRION Index and CONSCIENCE scores are derived from publicly accessible sources, including:

- Government monitoring agencies operating under public domain mandates (NOAA, NASA, USGS, ESA, FAO, NSIDC)
- Open-license scientific datasets (GBIF CC BY 4.0, Global Forest Watch CC BY 4.0, Copernicus open access)
- Public institutional databases (CDP public disclosure grades, SBTi public registry, Sabin Center public litigation database, InfluenceMap public company profiles)
- Open government regulatory filings (SEC EDGAR public submissions API — CORS-open, no license required)

### What VYRION Does Not Do

VYRION does not:
- Reproduce proprietary datasets in their original form
- Redistribute licensed data feeds from commercial data providers
- Sublicense data acquired under restricted terms
- Access non-public, paywalled, or licensed-only data products to produce its scores

VYRION scores are the product of VYRION's own analytical framework applied to publicly accessible inputs. The outputs — scores, bands, factor breakdowns — are VYRION's independent work product, not redistributions of source data.

### Commercial Data Providers

VYRION does not use commercial data feeds from Bloomberg, Refinitiv, MSCI, ISS ESG, Sustainalytics, or similar proprietary ESG data providers as inputs to its scoring. Where company names appear alongside these organizations in context (e.g., Bloomberg as a potential licensing partner), this refers to commercial relationships, not data sourcing.

## 3. Accuracy and Completeness

VYRION publishes scores in good faith based on the best available publicly accessible data. However:

- Publicly available data may contain errors, delays, or gaps that VYRION cannot independently verify
- Annual indicators may lag real-world conditions by up to 12 months
- Scores represent point-in-time assessments and are subject to revision when source data is corrected or updated
- The ±1.8 point uncertainty bound reflects documented measurement uncertainty propagated through the scoring model — it does not capture all potential sources of error

**Errors and corrections:** VYRION commits to publishing corrections within 24 hours of identification, as permanent entries in CHANGELOG.md. Find an error: contact@vyrion.earth.
