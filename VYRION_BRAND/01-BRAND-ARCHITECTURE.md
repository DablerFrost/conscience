## Color System — Unified Cyan (Confirmed April 2026)

--void:    #020912  (background — all platforms)
--glow:    #00D4FF  (electric cyan — primary accent)
--amber:   #FFB84D  (VYRION Index score display)
--breach:  #F03030  (breach band — CONSCIENCE)
--warn:    #F09000  (warning band — CONSCIENCE)
--t1:      #e4f2ff  (primary text)
--t2:      #6a9ab8  (secondary text, body copy)
--t3:      #2d6080  (tertiary text, labels)
--void2:   #061624  (card/cell backgrounds)
--border:  rgba(0,212,255,0.12) (all borders)

Previous green system (#030a05 / #00FF9D) is retired. Cyan is the single unified system across all modules: CONSCIENCE, ATLAS, SHIELD, and all future builds.

## Logo — 4 Locked Assets

1. Transparent PNG — use on any surface, headers, documents
2. Dark background JPG — primary mark, social media (Slide 1 on all carousels when used)
3. Designer 3D environment JPG — campaign posts, hero images
4. Icon only JPG — favicon, profile picture, app icon

Dark logo on dark background consistently outperforms other formats on social media. Lock dark logo as first image on any carousel or image post.

## Social Media — Role and Scope

Social media accounts exist to amplify featured content from the platform. They are not part of VYRION's architecture. The platform is the product. Social media is a distribution tool used selectively.

**Handles:**
X: @DablerFrost
Instagram: @DablerFrost
LinkedIn: linkedin.com/in/vyrion/

**What social media is used for:**
- The Record — weekly Wednesday issues
- Major VYRION Index movements (±1.0 or more)
- Platform milestones and module launches
- Institutional announcements
- Open Proof Protocol corrections
- Company-specific CONSCIENCE posts when score breakdowns are confirmed

**What social media is NOT used for:**
- Daily Index score cards
- Routine data updates that are already live on ATLAS
- Anything that simply restates what the platform already shows

The daily Index record lives at vyrion.earth/atlas.html — not on any social media account.
