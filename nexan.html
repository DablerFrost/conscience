Research Focus

What We're Working On

Nexan's research sits at three intersections. VYRION is the first proof of concept across all three. The Institute publishes corrections, not press releases.

PILLAR 01
AI-Human Alignment
What does it mean for an AI system to genuinely act in alignment with human values — not as a compliance exercise, but as a structural property of how the system is built? Nexan develops frameworks (Praxis) for evaluating alignment at the system architecture level, not just at the output level.
PILLAR 02
Accountability Infrastructure
Transparent, verifiable systems that measure the gap between what is promised and what is done. CONSCIENCE is the first operational proof of this pillar — 11 companies, 4 data sources, publicly auditable methodology, corrections published within 24 hours. The gap between pledge and reality is the signal.
PILLAR 03
Planetary Systems Thinking
The VYRION Index operationalizes planetary boundary science into a daily signal. Steffen et al. (2015), Richardson et al. (2023), and PIK Potsdam's boundary quantification form the scientific substrate. Nexan's contribution is translating Earth system science into infrastructure that institutional capital can act on.
PILLAR 04
Nex-Us Philosophy
The philosophical framework underlying all Nexan work. Nex-Us posits that intelligence — human, artificial, or collective — is not separate from the planetary systems it inhabits. It is embedded in them. The question is not whether AI affects planetary health, but whether it does so with awareness and accountability.
PILLAR 05
Transmedia Research
Ideas that shape the future need more than papers. Synthekai Collective translates Nexan's research questions into music, narrative, and transmedia work. The Nihilism Effect explores what it means to live inside systems that are measurably degrading. VYRION Index data, sonified as Mycelium Bloom.
PILLAR 06
Open Proof Protocol
Nexan publishes corrections, not retractions. Every VYRION score, every Index reading, every methodology assumption is challengeable. Find an error — we publish the correction within 24 hours as a permanent, timestamped changelog entry. No original entry is ever deleted. The protocol is the product.
